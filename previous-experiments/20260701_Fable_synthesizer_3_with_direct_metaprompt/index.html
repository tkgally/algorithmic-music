MURMURATION

A self-playing ensemble. Independent voices wander through a shared harmony — borrowing each other's phrases, moving with or against the crowd, drifting with the weather. You don't play notes; you shape their behavior.

MURMURATION

a self-playing ensemble — shape the flock, not the notes
Every voice chooses its next note by weighing the pull of the harmony, its appetite for leaps, what its neighbors just sang, and its urge to disagree. No two performances are the same. Try Contrarians high with Gravity high — orderly rebellion. Or Weave at maximum for rhythms that only realign once a minute.
