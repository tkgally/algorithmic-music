## [2026-07-11] update | Session 043 — background-mode: pre-render the next continuous track (no gap)

Tom reported a "quite long pause" between tracks in continuous background play (the render happening at the boundary; worse for longer pieces). Fixed by **double-buffering** in `docs/app.js`: while the current track plays, `prepareNext()` renders the next one (new seed, current style) to a ready WAV blob held in `prepared`, and `advanceRendered()` swaps it in instantly (falling back to on-demand render only if not ready). A `prepareToken` + the session `bg.seq` make superseded prepares (stop, or a style/seed change) discard and revoke themselves; `bgStop` drops the prepared blob too; the continuous toggle starts/discards the prepare when flipped mid-playback. Wiki: **updated [findings-comprehensive-site](findings-comprehensive-site.md)** ("Follow-up (session 043)" in the background-PoC section). Verified in-browser (continuous, short classical): the next track was pre-rendered ~10 s into the current one and the hand-off measured **11 ms** (was a multi-second gap), track-after-next preparing again, 0 errors. Additive, background-only; live engine, URL layout, determinism untouched. Gates: 197 node tests, `site-check` clean, `site-smoke` 49/49. Full narrative: `logs/sessions/043-2026-07-11.md`.

## [2026-07-11] update | Session 042 — background-mode: graceful track endings (fix) + platform-scope note

Tom tested the session-041 background PoC on his iPhone ("works pretty well") and reported one bug: in continuous background play the first few tracks ended on an **abrupt cut** instead of ringing out. Fix (`docs/app.js` `renderOffline`, background-only): a rendered file's length is `musicSec + tail`, and non-fade endings (cadence/stop/ringout) had no boundary shaping, so the file could end mid-reverb — the live engine hides this by overlapping the next piece in continuous play, but a standalone file has nothing after it. Gave the reverb a fuller tail (`0.6 + reverbSeconds·1.1`, was `0.4 + ·0.8`) and added a short safety fade to true silence over the final ~0.8 s for non-fade endings; verified the last 50–200 ms of rendered cadence pieces is now digital silence (peak 0) with the music level untouched. Wiki: **updated [findings-comprehensive-site](findings-comprehensive-site.md)** ("Follow-up (session 042)" in the background-PoC section) with the fix and a **platform-scope note** answering Tom's Android question — the live-engine background failure is worst on iOS but also affects Android (timer throttling), while the pre-render Background mode works on **both** iOS and Android (so it's not iOS-only; expose on mobile/touch devices, detectable via `pointer: coarse` + `maxTouchPoints`). Gates: 197 node tests, `site-check` clean, `site-smoke` 49/49. Full narrative: `logs/sessions/042-2026-07-11.md`.
