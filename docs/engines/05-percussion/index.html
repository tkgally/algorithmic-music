Percussion Ensemble engine 05

An ensemble of original drums, wood, and metal — nothing sampled, every hit synthesized live. Each seed picks its own structure and its own combination of instruments from a continuous space: a concert piece that develops a rhythmic cell to a climax, a folk ensemble anchored by an interlocking timeline and an improvising lead, a loose drum circle that swells and cuts — and everything in between. Press New piece to roam; open Structure & instruments to pin any dial yourself. Default 4/4; 6/8, 12/8, and the aksak 5/8 and 7/8 are there too.
