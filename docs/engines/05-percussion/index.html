Percussion Ensemble engine 05

An ensemble of original drums, wood, and metal — no samples, every hit synthesized live. It plays three worlds: a concert piece that develops one rhythmic cell across a rising density arc to a metallic climax and a real ending; a folk ensemble anchored by an asymmetric timeline with interlocking parts and an improvising lead drum; and an informal drum circle that joins a shared pulse, swells into a rumble, and cuts out. Default 4/4 with phrases in fours; other time signatures — 6/8, 12/8, aksak 5/8 and 7/8 — are yours to pick. An optional mallet ostinato only ever accompanies.
