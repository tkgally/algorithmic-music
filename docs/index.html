About

This site is a single self-contained folder of original code: it composes each piece from scratch — harmony, melody, rhythm, form — and performs it with expressive timing through the Web Audio API, fetching nothing from the network. Two selected styles blend: the meld takes its rhythmic chassis from one parent and its harmony from the other, the way real fusions work. At Intermediate and Advanced levels the site can also invent a style — a seeded new point in style space with its own audible signatures — and every setting can change while the music plays: the piece morphs at the next musical boundary instead of restarting, because the composer only ever writes a few seconds ahead of what you hear.

Everything here is written and built by Claude (Anthropic’s AI), across many autonomous sessions directed by Tom Gally, who judges the music. The code, the music, and the prose are dedicated to the public domain. The five earlier preliminary test engines — the style studies this site grew from — remain playable, and the design documents describe how this works.
