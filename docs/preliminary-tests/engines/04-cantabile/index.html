Cantabile engine 04

Cantabile — Italian for "in a singing style." A small ensemble of four original synthetic instruments that can bend, swell, and vibrate each note the way a violinist, a saxophonist, or an electric-guitar lead does. Every piece is a short modal arc that rises to a climax and resolves — and the players lean into it, expressing more where the music matters most. Synthesized live in your browser; nothing is a recording.
