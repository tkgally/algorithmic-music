### Deliverable 1 — whole-piece composer (`composePiece`)

Extended `experiments/composers/tonal-phrase.js` with `composePiece()` (leaving the tested `composePeriod()` untouched), reusing the period machinery (backward-Piston harmony, contour-first melody, pinned goal tones) and adding the three things Tom asked for, each grounded in `form-and-structure.md`:

- **Full form — intro + rounded ternary (A B A′) + coda**, 30 bars. A is the parallel period from session 017. **A′ restates A's exact harmony and on-beat melodic skeleton** (listeners track themes, not keys) — a true return. **B contrasts** by dropping register (`centerOffset` on the pins), opening off-tonic (vi/iv), using fresh material, and ending on the dominant (half cadence) to re-approach A′.
- **Motivic variation** — A′'s basic idea is **ornamented**: 1–2 interior quarters split into eighth-note pairs with an inserted passing/neighbor scale tone, on-beat pitches untouched (recognizable return, florid surface — diminution). Never touches the apex, so it stays the phrase's unique peak.
- **Ending (R6)** — a **coda**: whole-bar I–IV broadening, then an accelerated **mid-bar ii–V** (the one place harmony changes off the downbeat), resolving to a **lengthened final tonic**, with a melodic 3̂–2̂–1̂ descent to do (longest note, ending-tagged) and a head-motif recall. The performer adds a closing ritardando.

The composer also emits a per-section `intensity` so the performer can place the **climax at A′** (second half — a late climax, not a hard-coded ratio).

### Deliverable 2 — the audible layer (`synth`, `fx`) — shared-libraries build-order item 2

- **`experiments/lib/synth.js`** — three sample-free voices from stock nodes, each ramping its output gain from ~0 (anti-click) and disconnecting `onended`: an FM electric-piano lead (2-op, ratio 1:1, decaying index; velocity→brightness), a detuned-triangle string/pad (ensemble width from detuning, dark drifting lowpass), and a sine+triangle bass (mono, filtered). Triangles over saws for a soft, warm background palette (also renders cleaner).
- **`experiments/lib/fx.js`** — the shared master chain: per-voice buses (non-bass high-passed, lows mono/dry), one synthesized-IR convolution reverb (decaying channel-decorrelated noise, seed-deterministic, no samples), and saturation → glue compressor → safety limiter → trim with the loudness discipline (default undershoots to ≈ −11 dBFS RMS).

## Decisions and honest hedges left in place

- **`maxStep` for a full FM+ensemble+reverb mix is a regression bound, not an absolute click test.** Envelope click-safety is guaranteed by construction (every voice ramps from ~0) and separately *proven* by `render-measure.mjs` (0.02 safe vs 0.50 hard-gate). Band-limited-oscillator/FM timbre legitimately steps ~0.1–0.25; the engine gate (< 0.4, below the ~0.78 peak) catches a new gross discontinuity without re-litigating the guarantee. Documented in the harness and the finding.
- **A finite piece that stops is the point** (R6) — a "keep playing" toggle chains fresh seeds for continuous listening, but the default lets the ending be heard.
- **Softened the palette** (triangles, moderate FM index, default volume 0.6) for a warm background-classical sound that also renders cleaner — a deliberate taste + engineering call.
- **A′ restates harmony *and* on-beat melody; variation = ornamentation + dynamics** — keeps the return unmistakable while still developing it.
- **Minor-key cross-relation and inter-chord voice-leading are left as documented open items**, not papered over.
- **No OpenRouter spend.** All work built on already-verified wiki knowledge; Playwright stays a dev-time tool, never a repo dependency.
- **GitHub Pages is not enabled** — a one-time human step for Tom (Settings → Pages → `main`/`docs/`), noted in status + README.
