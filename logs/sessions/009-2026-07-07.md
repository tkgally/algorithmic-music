## Decisions / assumptions

- **Consolidated to one license file** rather than keeping two CC0 files. "Public domain for both" is most faithfully represented by a single `LICENSE` covering the whole project; two same-purpose CC0 files would be redundant and confusing. Removing `LICENSE-wiki` is reversible and all live references were updated (historical logs left as-is).
- **"Original, no outside code" reading:** interpreted as *don't copy/port another project's source*; reimplementing published algorithms and standard patterns in our own code is expected and allowed (with attribution). This is consistent with the existing `javascript-music-libraries.md` stance and is recorded there and in `shared-libraries.md`. Not filed as an open question for Tom — the natural reading is clear and low-stakes — but flagged in the pages so a future session doesn't over-read it.
- **Vendoring model** chosen to reconcile shared libraries with the self-containment rule: canonical source once in the repo, copied per engine, never fetched at runtime. Documented in both `engine-architecture.md` and `shared-libraries.md`.
- Trusted the verification subagents' sourced corrections (they fetched the cited sources); the numeric fixes on expressive-performance were the session's highest-value catch and all had a stated source for the correct value.
