### Deliverable 1 — `experiments/composers/tonal-phrase.js` (worked R1 + R2)

A reference **Composer** (engine-level, not a shared library) that writes one eight-bar **parallel period** deterministically from a seed, emitting the [engine-architecture](../../wiki/engine-architecture.md) Note-event schema `{ beat, durBeats, midi, voice, role, tags }` plus a `selfReport` of intent. Design, all grounded in the wiki:

- **R2 (harmony):** cadence-first backward generation. Fix the cadence chord (antecedent → V for a half cadence; consequent → V→I for a PAC), pin bar 1 to I, then fill the interior by sampling each predecessor from a **reversed Piston table** weighted by forward probability (`theory.pistonSuccessors` + `rng.weighted`). Finding: pre-dominant-before-dominant falls out for free — IV and ii are Piston's two "usually → V" motions, so walking backward from V surfaces them as the most common predecessors, no special-case rule. Minor keys use a fixed functional skeleton with a harmonic-minor (leading-tone) V, honestly flagged because Piston's table is major-only.
- **R1 (melody):** contour-first. Fix the cadence goal tone (re/sol at the HC, do at the PAC — the max-stability pitch class) and one apex first, target an arch contour, then fill scale tones by a constraint-weighted local choice: chord tones on strong beats, step bias, post-leap reversal (~0.7), regression-to-mean, "leaps up steps down," and a stepwise cadential approach. The consequent restates the antecedent's basic idea (parallel period). Non-apex notes are capped below the apex so it stays the phrase's unique peak.

15 structural tests (`experiments/tests/composer.test.js`) verify determinism, the harmonic goals, the parallel-period restatement, diatonic melody, the cadential goal tones (PAC note = Krumhansl max), the unique tagged apex, a step-dominated interval budget (≈71% steps / ≈7% leaps), and interior post-leap reversal (≈68%). A real side-finding surfaced and is recorded: **the arch contour competes with the reversal rule** along its monotonic limbs, and the two intentional structural leaps per phrase (into the apex, into the cadence) are non-reversing by design — so the reversal statistic is measured on interior leaps, where it matches von Hippel & Huron's ~72%.

### Deliverable 2 — OfflineAudioContext render-and-measure harness

Three parts:

- **`experiments/lib/analysis.js`** — the pure PCM-measurement core of the planned `analysis` library and the harness's measurement half: `rms`, `peak`, `dbfs`/`rmsDbfs`, `maxStep` (click proxy), `detectOnsets` (amplitude, blips-from-silence), `detectOnsetsEnergy` (trailing-median energy rise, for sustained beds), `longestSilenceSec`, `onsetTimingError`. No Web Audio in the module, so it is unit-tested in Node against synthesized signals (8 tests) before being trusted on browser renders.
- **`experiments/demos/offline-render.html`** — vendors all four libs + the composer as classic scripts; exposes `window.__render()`; builds an `OfflineAudioContext`, renders (a Euclidean timing pattern, an unsafe click control, or a composed period), and measures with `analysis`. Also a visible `file://` demo (buttons + waveform + JSON report).
- **`experiments/tools/render-measure.mjs`** — Playwright driver (same dev-time access pattern as `verify.mjs`), applies acceptance gates, exits non-zero on failure.

Results (`node experiments/tools/render-measure.mjs` → 12/12 gates): `transport`'s beats→seconds math is **sample-accurate** — all onsets detected, jitter **0.015 ms** (≈0.7 sample at 44.1 kHz), max error **0.24 ms**. The click-safe envelope is genuinely click-safe: `maxStep` **0.018** vs **0.50** for a deliberately click-inducing control (~27×, so the proxy discriminates). The composed period renders clean (no clipping, no silent gap, −25 dBFS). Honest limitation recorded: full-mix onset recovery is approximate (17/24 melody notes vs 20/20 for blips from silence) — spectral flux is the documented next step — so the harness hard-gates onset *accuracy* on the controlled timing test and treats the full-mix onset *count* as a diagnostic.

## Decisions and honest hedges left in place

- **The composer is engine-level, not a shared library** — deliberately placed in `experiments/composers/`, not `experiments/lib/`, because composition is policy that consumes the primitives, not a reusable primitive itself.
- **Major mode is the fully Piston-driven demonstration; minor uses a fixed skeleton** with a harmonic-minor dominant, flagged rather than dressed up as general. The natural-minor-melody-over-harmonic-minor-V cross-relation is left as a documented listening-check item.
- **The offline harness validates timing math and click-safety, not the live-clock loop.** Real-time drift / hidden-tab throttling against a live `AudioContext` is non-deterministic and not measurable offline; it stays a separate manual/field check (the `euclid-transport.html` demo).
- **Full-mix onset detection is a diagnostic, not a precision claim** — spectral flux / per-voice tapping is the documented fix.
- **No OpenRouter spend.** All work was building/testing against existing verified wiki knowledge; no second-opinion model calls needed. Playwright and (from session 016) Tonal remain dev-time-only tools, never repo dependencies.
