## Decisions made (working assumptions, Tom can override)

1. **Determinism is redefined honestly at the audio level.** Chromium sums a node's multiple inputs in an unspecified order; float addition is not associative; two renders of the same graph can differ by ~1 ulp (~1.0e-7, ≈ −140 dBFS), intermittently. Byte-identical PCM is therefore **not a browser-attainable target** for fan-in graphs. The site's guarantee: the symbolic event stream is byte-identical (Node-asserted), and rendered audio is identical within float rounding (gate: max sample diff ≤ 1e-5). Bisection evidence in `wiki/findings-comprehensive-site.md`.
2. **Meld ensembles keep the chassis parent's rhythm-section roles and the harmony parent's pitched roles**; strategies resolve roles with fallbacks (`comp`→`pad`, `bass`→`drone`, `lead`→`counter`) so any genre's strategy can play any meld ensemble.
3. **Invented styles route to an existing strategy family** by their drawn fields, with hostability repairs (ambient needs a sustaining role; groove strategies get their home meters; aksak/compound moves to folk). Signatures are realized centrally (motto→motif, interval-cell scoring bias, voicing habits, echo tail) so every strategy inherits them; `cadenceDrop` is defined but deferred.
4. **The live swap starts the incoming style from its top** under the crossfade (the outgoing fades over ~2 bars); the URL always carries the destination snapshot (Tom's rule).
5. Volume is UI-local (not serialized); the seed displays as 8-hex (any text input is hashed).

## What was learned (filed in `wiki/findings-comprehensive-site.md`)

The JIT loop works and units-as-phrases is the right grain (rubato, boundary changes, and per-unit streams all fall out); one vector-driven composer core genuinely serves presets, melds, and inventions; notes may outlive their unit (the performer's time map must extend); the accompaniment grid is the chord slot, not the bar; fan-in PCM nondeterminism (above); and the orchestration pattern — a written contract + a cheap self-serve gate — let six parallel pack authors land without interface drift.
